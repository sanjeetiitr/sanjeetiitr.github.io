About Us

Welcome to Matrix!

We are a coaching institute for JEE main & advanced located in a small town Sikar in north-eastern Rajasthan. We believe that we provide better education than any other institute in Rajasthan or India. 8 faculty members at Matrix are from IITs, another 3 from NITs - something unheard in past. We have had a very successful reasult season in 2015 and roaring to do even better in 2016. For more info, please visit us.
