RESULT

2014-15 result summary: We had a stupendous result season in 2015. The performance looks even brighter when accounted for the fact that this was our first ever result for JEE. All in all, 176 out of 225 appearing students qualified JE main in 2015. Highest marks in JEE main were scored by Jatin sharma (251 marks). Incidently, this was also the second highest no. scored by any student from coaching in Sikar. The highest marks being just 252 by another student from another coaching institute.

51 full time Matrix students qualified JEE advanced in 2015. Considering we had only 225 students appearing in JEE main, on a % basis, our success rate has been many times more than comparable coaching institute in Kota.

About Us

Welcome to Matrix!

We are a coaching institute for JEE main & advanced located in a small town Sikar in north-eastern Rajasthan. We believe that we provide better education than any other institute in Rajasthan or India. 8 faculty members at Matrix are from IITs, another 3 from NITs - something unheard in past. We have had a very successful reasult season in 2015 and roaring to do even better in 2016. For more info, please visit us.
