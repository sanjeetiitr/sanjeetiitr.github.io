Faculty of Mathematics


Following section provides a brief description of our faculty members:

OP DHAKA

Physics

OM Dhaka is B.Tech from IIT Roorkee. Before co-founding Matrix JEE academy, he was a senior faculty of physics with Akash institute mainly focusing on JEE advanced. He is teaching exclusively for IIT-JEE students for about last 6 years. Before joining Matrix he patronized the Department of Physics at Aakash institute and was regarded in high esteem by students for his sharp explanatory and analytical skills. Om himself has been a brilliant student during school and college. He successfully cracked IIT-JEE examination in his first attempt (during class 12th).

ANUPAM AGARWAL

Physics

Anumpam Agarwal is a B.Tech from prestigious IIT Kanpur. Anupam qualified in IIT-JEE 2006 in general category & joined IIT Kanpur for a four year program in Mechanical engineering. Before joining Matrix JEE academy as a physics faculty, Anupam taught extensively at Allen Kota for IIT JEE. Anupam graduated from IIT Kanpur in 2010 and till date has a cumulative work experience of more than 4 years. Anupam was an extremely popular faculty at ALLEN among student community and was widely known for his novel methods to simplify complex mechanics problems.

KULDEEP KHICHAR

Physics

Kuldeep is a B.Tech from Punjab engineering college. Right out of college, he joined Jindal Steel, one of the largest steel companies in India as a process engineer. He worked with Jindal Steel in Karnataka for ~3 years and received many award for outstanding contribution to quality improvement group. But soon over, he quit the corporate role for his interest in teaching. He joined Matrix in the very first year in 2014 and has been a key member of the core group of faculties now.
