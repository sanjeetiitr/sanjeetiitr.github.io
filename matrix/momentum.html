Course Concept and Structure

JEE (main & advanced both)

Class 10th to 11th moving students

Multiple batches start during April to July months; Contact our office for exact dates

Separate batches for English & Hindi medium

Direct admission basis 10th performance (minimum 70% marks in 10th)

Most recommended course for students because it provides ample time for a relaxed and comprehensive preparation schedule for JEE. Comfortable speed of syllabus during 2 years. Primary focus of this course is to develop solid foundation of & fundamentals. Secondary focus is to sharpen analytical thinking, reasoning and problem solving skills.
This course also has undivided attention towards board preparation to ensure stupendous performance in 12th class

First Year (During class 11th) : 11th class syllabus will be taught to the students from competitive exam and school point of view both. Syllabus for class 11this scheduled to be finished by mid of January. Post this, students would be free to attend revision classes as per individual requirements. During this entire period, faculty members remain available for individual doubt removal and other academic support as per student needs.

Second Year (During class 12th) : The syllabus of 12thclass (from competitive exam and board point of view) will be completed by end of September. Complete revision of class 11th syllabus and 12th board preparation by end of December. After this, we keep conducting revision classes, revision test series, major test series and Problem Solving Counters for students till final examination.

School Preparation : Syllabus of board will also be covered to ensure good marks in board exam. Classes for English are also conducted for school/Board examination. Physics & Chemistry practicals will also be covered.
