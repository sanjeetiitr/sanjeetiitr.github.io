Course Concept and Structure

JEE (main & advanced both)

Students who have recently passed 12th

Multiple batches start during April to July months; Contact our office for exact dates

Separate batches for English & Hindi medium

Direct admission basis 10th & 12th performance

Recommended for those students have developed some fundamentals during 11th & 12th, but haven't done enough to crack prestigious JEE advanced. The course covers both 11th & 12th syllabus. The primary focus of this course is to develop problem solving skills to enable students get through JEE advanced with good rank. The entire syllabus would be completed by end of December. Once the course is completed, we keep conducting revision classes, revision test series, major test series and Problem Solving Counters for students till final examination.
