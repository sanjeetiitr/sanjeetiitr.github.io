Course Concept and Structure

JEE (main & advanced both)

Class 11th to 12th moving students

Two batches start during April & May months; Contact our office for exact dates

English & Hindi

Direct admission basis 10th & 11th performance

Recommended for those students have developed some fundamentals during 11th class, but aren't feeling confident about cracking JEE advanced & main. The course covers 12th syllabus from both JEE and board point of view. However, 11th class syllabus is mostly covered from refining problem solving skills. The primary focus of this course is to develop problem solving skills to enable students get through JEE advanced with good rank. In addition to competitive exam preparation, this course also takes care of School/Board Examination. The syllabus of 12thclass (from competitive exam and board point of view) will be completed by end of October/november. Revision of class 11th syllabus is done simultaneously while completing 12th class syllabus. Once the 12th syllabus is completed, there would be regular revision classes, review tests, revi and problem solving & doubt clearance session for students till JEE advanced .
