EmagenTV is a powerful and easy to use Android Based professional digital signage platform.

Converts your everyday Android devices such as Phones, Tablets and TVs to digital signage that communicates to your audience with real-time dynamic content.

Running on the cloud, we can help you manage one or thousands of screens with no effort. All tasks are directly performed on your browser, so there is no need for large downloads or complicated software / hardware installs.

1. Drag-and-drop timeline interface
2. Create as many zones in your screen as you want
3. Several Apps for presenting dynamic contents like social media, weather, RSS feeds, streaming media and much more.
4. Use HTML5 to create your own Apps
5. Remotely access and control your screens right from the browser

Showcase the content you want

EmagenTV is your digital content creator with our easy to use layout manager allow you to show the largest variety of content within minutes. Unlock real-time social media, news feeds, local weather conditions, dashboards, and much more on any display. Our software also comes with automatic updates and security monitoring. Turn your Android device or TV into your own channel, displaying exactly what you want when you want it.

Customize your display on the cloud.

EmagenTV gives you control over all your displays from any computer. Easily enable apps, craft themes, schedule graphics, and much more on our online web dashboard.
