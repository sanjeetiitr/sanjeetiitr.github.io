Download

Get EmagenTV For your device.

EmagenTV for Android comes in different versions depending on your device. Selecting the right version of your device will ensure that you get access to all features EmagenTV has to offer.

System App

If your device has a CPU from Rockchip, Amlogic or Telechips, you should use EmagenTV’s System App. This includes most devices from Minix, Geniatech, Rickomagic and others. The System App will give you access to all our features out of the box. If you are not sure whether your device is one of the above, all you have to do is try to install the System App. If your device is not compatible with it, the installation will give you an error message and you can proceed to install our Standard App.

Download System App 5.0.1

Standard App

The standard version of EmagenTV should work on any device using Android 4.0.4 and up. This version does not have any special hardware integration and in order to benefit from features like Remote View and Scheduled Reboots your device will need to be "rooted". If your device is rooted you should install this version. You will be able to manage updates using EmagenTV built-in Remote Update. If your device is not rooted and not compatible with the System App, you should install the Play Store App below.

Download Standard App 5.0.2

Play Store App

This is EmagenTV available on Google Play Store. It works just like the Standard App, except for app updates. When installed through the Play Store, you must enable Automatic Updates on the Play Store app to update EmagenTV. Please mind that Play Store updates might take a couple of weeks to be applied. If you are unsure of which app version to install, contact our support team and we will be glad to help.
